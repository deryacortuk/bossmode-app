# BossMode

**Press a button. Say everything you can’t.**

BossMode is a tiny Windows app that lets you release your real thoughts privately — during meetings, coding sessions, studying, or any moment you can’t react out loud.

It shows messages **only on your screen** for a few seconds.
No one else sees anything. Ever.

---

## Download

👉 https://github.com/deryacortuk/bossmode-app/releases/latest/download/BossMode.exe

* Windows 10 / 11
* ~2 MB


---

## How it works

1. Download `BossMode.exe`
2. Double-click to run
3. Press your rage button 😤
4. A message appears on your screen (only yours)
5. It disappears after a few seconds

That’s it.

---

## What it does

* Shows short reaction messages on your screen
* Works on top of any app (Zoom, Teams, browser, IDE, games)
* Lets you express what you’re thinking — privately

---

## What it does NOT do

* No internet connection
* No tracking
* No analytics
* No data collection
* No background processes
* No system changes

It’s a simple local overlay. Nothing more.

---

## Privacy

BossMode is **100% offline**.

* No network calls
* No logs
* No screenshots
* No data leaves your machine

What you see stays with you.

---

## Important (Windows warning)

When you run the app for the first time, Windows may show:

> “Windows protected your PC”

This happens because the app is not code-signed yet.

To run it:

* Click **More info**
* Click **Run anyway**
